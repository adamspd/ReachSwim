How packages work

Credits are locked to the session type and pool you buy them for — that's because lane hire costs differ per venue. When you're logged in, credits apply automatically at checkout. If you ever book while logged out, you'll have individual codes in your confirmation email to enter manually.

Each package has its own validity window shown on the package card. Unused credits can't be refunded once that window has passed.
